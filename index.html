A little about me...

Hi I'm Martin!

Hi my name is Martin and I'm an aspiring front end developer! I recently found out about web development and thought I would give it a go, I certainly did not think I would enjoy it this much! I now love coding, the challenge of it, the creativity, the design, and the reward I get from it are all reasons why I'm drawn to it. I also love learning new things about coding and development and I'm excited to become a master. I have struggled for a while to find an occupation I enjoy doing and it seems coding has come in at the perfect time in my life.

My Dream

My dream is to work as a developer for a progressive comapany full of knowledgeable people to learn from.// I beleive that its important to do something your passionate about and thats what web development.// I beleive that working as a developer will fulfill my needs in doing something im passionate about. I see myself fitting in well in the developer work environment and am excited to learn as much as i can.
